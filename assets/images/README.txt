Drop your project thumbnails and hero images here.

Naming convention used by the example projects:
  - matrix-resurrections.jpg         (thumbnail, ~1200x750, 16:10)
  - matrix-resurrections-hero.jpg    (hero, ~2400x1200)
  - barbaren-s2.jpg
  - stranger-things-s3.jpg
  - ...etc

Any filename works — just make sure the `thumbnail:` and `hero_image:`
frontmatter fields in your _projects/*.md files match.
