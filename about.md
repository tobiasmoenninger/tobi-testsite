      <p class="page-kicker">About</p>
      <h1>Tobias Moenninger</h1>
    </header>

    <div class="prose">
      <p>
        Tobias was born in 1985 in Marburg an der Lahn, Germany. After watching
        through the whole library of his local video store as a young teenager he
        quickly realized that the moving picture business is the only business he
        wants to partake in on a professional level.
      </p>
      <p>
        He studied Media Technology (B.Eng.) and Media Computer Science (M.Sc.) at
        the University of Applied Sciences Düsseldorf. During that time he started
        working as a Videographer, Editor, Digital Image Technician, and Compositing
        Artist on various commercial and feature film productions. In 2009 his name
        appeared in the rolling credits of a feature film for the first time as a
        Compositing Artist.
      </p>
      <p>
        Over the following years he moved to Berlin, joining studios including
        RISE Visual Effects Studios, MovieBrats Studios, and Pixomondo, working as a
        Compositing Artist and eventually Compositing Supervisor on productions such
        as <em>Shazam!</em>, <em>Stranger Things</em>, and <em>Iron Sky: The Coming
        Race</em>. He then relocated to Sydney to join Animal Logic as a Senior
        Compositing Artist on <em>Peter Rabbit 2</em> and <em>The Matrix
        Resurrections</em>.
      </p>
      <p>
        He currently works as a VFX Supervisor on feature films and series,
        with recent credits including <em>Der junge Häuptling Winnetou</em> (SamFilm)
        and <em>Barbaren: Staffel 2</em> (Gaumont / Netflix). He is based in
        Lüneburg, Germany, and works internationally.
      </p>
      <p>
        His primary tools are Nuke X (since 2009), Maya, DaVinci Resolve, After
        Effects, and Premiere Pro. He holds a firm belief that "no" should never be
        the only option.
      </p>
